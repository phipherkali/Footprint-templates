# Institutional Order Flow Footprint Templates

A curated collection of advanced order flow and volume footprint templates optimized for institutional-grade market analysis. These configurations are designed specifically to enhance clarity regarding market mechanics, aggressive market orders, delta distribution, and liquidity pools.

## Supported Platforms
* **ATAS** (Order Flow Trading Platform)
* **ExoCharts** (Advanced Cluster Charting Software)

---

## 📊 Included Templates

### 1. ATAS Order Flow Configs
* Optimized for tracking real-time delta imbalances and auction market mechanics.
* High-visibility layouts for tracking volume clusters and aggressive market buying/selling tails.

### 2. ExoCharts Cluster Profiles
* Advanced footprint configurations designed for deep-dive delta footprint analysis.
* Fine-tuned color grading for optimal visual tracking of volume profiles, VWAP deviations, and point of control (POC) transitions.

## 🛠️ Installation & Setup

### For ATAS:
1. Copy the downloaded `.xml` or template files.
2. Navigate to your ATAS root directory (usually `Documents\ATAS\Templates\` or `Snapshots\`).
3. Paste the files into the designated folder.
4. Open ATAS, right-click your chart, select **Templates**, and load the configuration.

### For ExoCharts:
1. Open ExoCharts and navigate to the **Template Manager**.
2. Click **Import** and select the configuration files from this package.
3. Apply the template directly to your desired asset ticker (Gold, Crude Oil, or Equity Index Futures).
